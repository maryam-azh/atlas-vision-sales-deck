# Atlas Vision
 Automated Intelligence for Automotive Pre-Delivery Inspection  


We build intelligent technologies to automatically **detect, classify, and document vehicle defects** for OEMs, dealers, and insurers.  



#### Impact Statement  
We expect to reduce inspection time by 70 %, improving defect accuracy to 97 %, and cutting inspection costs by half.  


---

#### 1. Problem: Manual inspections are inconsistent, costly, and slow. 
> **50 M+ vehicles inspected manually each year — each one a potential missed defect.**

#### Key Pain Points
- **Inconsistent results:** Human inspections vary by technician and lighting — up to **30 % of defects go undetected**, causing warranty claims and rework.  
- **Delivery bottlenecks:** Each inspection takes **20–30 minutes per vehicle**, delaying throughput at factories, yards, and dealerships.  
- **High cost of errors:** Missed defects or unnecessary repairs cost OEMs and insurers **hundreds of millions annually**.  
- **No digital traceability:** Most inspection data is still on paper or siloed systems — **no analytics, no accountability**.

#### 4. Product: Edge-first Agentic Platform for AI-Driven Vehicle Inspection and Quality Intelligence.

#### breakdown
| **Stage** | **Action** | **Output / Value** |
|------------|-------------|--------------------|
| **1. Capture** | Vehicles are scanned automatically using mobile devices, fixed rigs, or drones. | High-quality visual and sensor data tagged to each VIN. |
| **2. Detect** | AI identifies scratches, dents, misalignments, and missing parts in real time. | Instant defect localization with accuracy above 95 %. |
| **3. Reason** | AI classifies defect severity and recommends corrective actions. | Clear defect summary with actionable next steps. |
| **4. Verify & Learn** | Technicians review flagged cases; feedback improves accuracy. | Human-AI collaboration loop that builds trust and precision. |
| **5. Report & Integrate** | Results sync automatically to OEM, dealer, or insurer systems. | **Digital Vehicle Passport** with complete inspection history. |
